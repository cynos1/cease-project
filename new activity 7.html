What happens to my health after quitting? ↓

  • After 20 minutes

    Heart rate and blood pressure drop.

  • After 12 hours

    a highly poisonous gas level in your blood drops to normal.

  • After 2 weeks

    Blood flow improves.
    Walking becomes easier for you.
    Your lung function increases up to 30%.

  • After 1 month

    Lung It works to keep the airways clear of mucus and dirt regain normal function.
    Ability to clear lungs of mucus increases.
    Coughing, fatigue, shortness of breath decrease.

  • After 1 year

    Excess risk of chronic heart disease (CHD) lowers to 1/2 prior amount.

  • After 5 years

    Risk of stroke is reduced to that of people who have never smoked.

  • After 10 years

    Lung cancer death rate drops to half that of a continuing smoker.
    Risk of cancer decreases.
    Including mouth, throat, esophagus, bladder, kidney, pancreatic cancers.

  • After 15 years

    Risk of Chronic heart disease is like that of people who have never smoked.
