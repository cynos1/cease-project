How can quitting improve your health? ↓

  • After 20 Minutes

    Heart rate and blood pressure drop.

  • After 12 Hours

    a highly poisonous gas level in your blood drops to normal.

  • After 2 Weeks

    Blood flow improves.
    Walking becomes easier.
    Your lung function increases up to 30%.

  • After 1 Month

    Lung It works to keep the airways clear of mucus and dirt regain normal function.
    Ability to clear lungs increases.
    Coughing, fatigue, shortness of breath decrease.

  • After 1 Year

    Excess risk of Chronic Heart Disease (CHD) lowers by 50%.

  • After 5 Years

    Risk of stroke is the same as people who have never smoked.

  • After 10 Years

    Risk of dying from lung cancer reduces by 50%.
    Risks of other cancers also decrease.

  • After 15 Years

    Risk of Chronic Heart Disease is like that of people who have never smoked.
