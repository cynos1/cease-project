GUM

Products

  • Nicorette, Thrive, Generic Available OTC 2mg, 4mg

    • Advantages

  • Convenient
  • Flexible Dosing
  • Fast onset of delivery
  • Available without prescription

    • Disadvantages

  • Cannot eat or drink while using the gum
  • Daily use during the day required to obtain adequate nicotine levels
  • Avoid with dental problems and TMJ

    • LOZENGE

    Products

  • Nicorette Lozenge, Nicorette Mini Lozenge, Commit Lozenge Generic Available OTC 2mg, 4mg

    • Advantages

  • Convenient
  • Flexible Dosing
  • Fast onset of delivery
  • Available without prescription

    • Disadvantages

  • Gastrointestinal side effects (nausea, hiccups, heartburn)
  • Daytime use during the day required to obtain adequate nicotine levels

    • PATCH

    Products

  • Nicoderm CQ, Prostep, Habitrol, Generic Available OTC 7mg, 14mg, 21mg (24-hour release)

    • Advantages

  • Easy to use
  • Requires Daily application
  • Few side effects
  • Available without prescription

    • Disadvantages

  • Less flexible dosing
  • Slow Onset of delivery
  • Mild skin rashes and irritation

    • NASAL SPRAY

    Products

  • Nicotrol NS, Rx Required Metered spray, 0.5mg nicotine in 50mcl

    • Advantages

  • Flexible Dosing
  • Fastest delivery of Nicotine of currently available products
  • Reduces cravings in minutes

    • Disadvantages

  • Nose and eye irritation is common but usually disappears within one week
  • Frequent daytime use required to obtain adequate nicotine levels

    • ORAL INHALER

    Products

  • Nicotrol Inhaler, Rx Required 10mg Cartridge 4mg

    • Advantages

  • Flexible Dosing
  • Mimics behaviour of smoking
  • Few side effects
  • Fast onset of delivery

    • Disadvantages

  • May cause mouth or throat irritation
  • Daytime required to obtain adequate nicotine levels

    • Bupropion-SR

    Product

  • ZybanTM. Generic Rx Required 150g tablet

    • Advantages

  • Easy to Use
  • Pill form
  • Can be used in combination with NRT
  • May benefit those with depression
  • May delay weight gain

    • Disadvantages

  • Avoid with certain health conditions
  • Lack of flexibility of use.

    • Varenicline


    Products

  • ChantixTM Rx Required 0.5mg, 1mg tablet

    • Advantages

  • Easy to use
  • Pill form
  • Reduces cravings by blocking the pleasurable effects of nicotine in the brain.

    • Disadvantages

  • Avoid with certain health conditions
  • Lack of flexibility of use
  • May induce nausea
