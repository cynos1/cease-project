How should you pick a quit date?

people are more successful in quitting when they set a target such as selecting a day that they intend to quit. Choose a date as soon as possible. A month works well because you have enough time to prepare yourself to quit but not enough time to talk yourself out of it. It's best to let your quit buddy, family and friends know of your quit date.

How should you pick a quit date?







Some people prefer to quit on a specific day marking a special event as a gift to themselves

Some examples include:

  • Your birthday, make it a gift to yourself
  • Your wedding anniversary, make it a gift to your partner
  • New year's day; New year New resolution
  • Independence day; celebrate your upcoming independence from nicotine

    • For others, these special events/days might come with celebrations or memories that pose difficlties to quitting therefore you should find what works for you in selecting your quit date. You may prefer to quit the day after the special event if that works better for you.
