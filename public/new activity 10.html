Benefits of Quitting


Why should you quit?

You'll see physical benefits


Your breath will smell better, you'll have whiter teeth, your fingernails will not yellow, and you'll regain your sense of smell and taste.

You'll save money


In addition to saving money on cigarettes, you'll save on medical costs.

You'll set a good example


Children whose parents smoke are more likely to start smoking themselves. You can become a good role model for them by quitting now.

You'll be more welcome


  • Reduced secondhand smoke around friends and family
  • Less health-related costs
  • Smoke-free locations restaurants, bars, apartment buildings etc.
