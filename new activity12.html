Support to quit

A Quit Buddy offers help with:

Attention 

A quit buddy should be a patient listener who is readily available when you need help.

   
Distraction

A quit buddy should help when you need a distraction to cope with tobacco cravings.

   
Motivation

A quit buddy should support you by continuing to express confidence in your ability to quit.

   
Reassurance

A quit buddy should continue to support you beyond the first few weeks of quitting by watching your progress and congratulating you on any positive changes you make.

   
Accountability

A quit buddy can help lower your stress by providing advance notice of work deadlines or helping with chores or other responsibilities.
